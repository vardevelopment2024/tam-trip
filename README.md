# Tam Trip

**Your personal travel companion for planning and tracking trips.**

Tam Trip is a mobile app for iOS and Android that helps you plan, organize, and navigate your travels. Whether you're exploring a new city or road-tripping across the country, Tam Trip keeps all your trip details in one place.

## Features

- **Trip Planning** — Create trips manually or let the app build smart itineraries for you
- **Interactive Maps** — Explore destinations with integrated maps (Apple Maps on iOS, Google Maps on Android)
- **Place Discovery** — Search and bookmark places you want to visit
- **Route Building** — Get directions between your planned stops with optimized routing
- **Travel Mode** — Track your journey in real-time with background location support
- **Bookmarks** — Save your favorite places for future trips
- **Multi-language** — Available in English, Spanish, and Russian
